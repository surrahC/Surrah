About Garlic Tomato BBQ Ingredients Process

FI Fish Chips brings you an innovative snack experience combining the nutritional benefits of freshwater fish with irresistible flavors. Our chips are made from premium Puntius fish, carefully processed to preserve their natural goodness while delivering exceptional crunch. Each bite offers a perfect balance of health and taste, making them ideal for health-conscious snack lovers who don't want to compromise on flavor.

Proposed Garlic Flavour

Enjoy the crispiness with the punch of real garlic. 100% organic and irresistibly delicious!

Our Garlic Flavor Fish Chips are crafted with freshly crushed garlic and a blend of aromatic herbs that create a bold yet balanced taste profile. The garlic is slow-roasted to perfection before being incorporated into our special seasoning mix, ensuring a deep, rich flavor without overpowering the natural taste of the fish. These chips pair wonderfully with dips or can be enjoyed on their own as a satisfying snack any time of day.

The packaging for our Garlic Flavor is designed to preserve freshness while being environmentally conscious. Each bag contains a single serving of protein-rich fish chips that provide energy and satisfaction. The resealable feature ensures your snacks stay crispy till the last bite. Perfect for lunchboxes, road trips, or as a post-workout recovery snack.

Proposed Tomato Flavour

Tangy and tasty tomato burst with every bite. Perfect for snack time!

The Tomato Flavor Fish Chips feature sun-ripened tomatoes that are carefully processed to capture their natural tanginess and sweetness. Combined with a hint of basil and oregano, these chips offer a Mediterranean-inspired taste experience. The tomato powder we use is made through a special dehydration process that locks in nutrients and flavor, resulting in a snack that's as wholesome as it is delicious.

Our Tomato Flavor packaging stands out with its vibrant red design, making it easy to spot on shelves. The portion-controlled packs help with mindful snacking while providing a good source of protein and omega-3 fatty acids from the fish. These chips are school-safe (nut-free) and make an excellent alternative to traditional potato chips for those seeking more nutritional value from their snacks.

Barbecue Flavour

Smoky, savory, and crunchy. Barbecue chips just got a bold twist!

The Barbecue Flavor Fish Chips deliver a smoky, slightly sweet taste with just the right amount of heat. We use a special blend of paprika, onion powder, and natural smoke flavor to create that authentic barbecue taste. Unlike traditional barbecue snacks that can be overly salty, our version maintains a balanced flavor profile that lets the quality of the fish shine through. These are perfect for game nights or as a unique addition to your party snack spread.

The Barbecue Flavor comes in eye-catching packaging that promises adventure for your taste buds. We've designed the bag to be sturdy enough to prevent crushing while being lightweight for easy carrying. The larger family-size package is perfect for sharing, or you can opt for the single-serve packs for portion control. Either way, you're getting a snack that's high in protein and low in empty calories compared to conventional chips.

How It will Make

Craft with care and inspired by coastal freshness. At the heart of every bite is Puntius fish, a small yet flavorful freshwater catch known for its tender texture and rich taste. Sourced sustainably, these fish are cleaned, filleted, and transformed into golden, crispy delights.

We have intent to fry our chips in pure sunflower oil, ensuring a light and healthy crunch that does not weigh you down.

Garlic Glaze: Rich, roast garlic with a hint of lemony zing.
Tomato Zest: Tangy sun-dried tomato with a subtle herby note.
Smoky BBQ: Deep, smoky spices with a touch of sweetness and fire.

To boost both taste and nutrition, we try to sprinkle in a blend of healthy spices such as turmeric (anti-inflammatory), black cumin (kalonji) for its antioxidant kick, and a dash of moringa powder for a superfood twist.

Our production process maintains strict hygiene standards while preserving the nutritional integrity of the ingredients. The fish is processed within hours of catch to ensure maximum freshness, then carefully prepared, seasoned, and packaged to deliver premium quality in every bag.
